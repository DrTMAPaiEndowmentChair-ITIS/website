## Project Overview

This project involved exploratory research in agile AI model development, focusing on cascaded super learning. It was sponsored by Schneider Electric, BGRT, Bengaluru.

### Funding Details
- **Funding Agency**: Schneider Electric, BGRT, Bengaluru
- **Sanctioned Date**: January 2024
- **Amount**: Rs. 12 Lakhs

### Research Team
- **Principal Investigator**: Dr. Manohara Pai M.M
- **Co-Investigators**:
  - Dr. Rohini Rao (Data Science and Computer Applications)
  - Dr. Radhika M Pai (Data Science and Computer Applications)
- **Students**: 4 students (DSCA and ICT)

## Technology Implementation

This exploratory research focused on developing:

- **Cascaded Super Learning Architecture** for enhanced model performance
- **Ensemble Methods** that combine multiple base learners
- **Transfer Learning Techniques** to leverage pre-trained knowledge
- **Optimization Algorithms** for efficient model training

### Key Innovations

The project delivered several significant advancements in AI model development:

| Feature | Benefit |
|:--------|:--------|
| Cascaded Architecture | Hierarchical learning that reduces error propagation |
| Dynamic Ensembling | Adaptively selects optimal combination of base learners |
| Automated Hyperparameter Tuning | Reduces manual configuration requirements |
| Cross-domain Knowledge Transfer | Leverages learning from related domains |
| Incremental Learning | Adapts models with minimal retraining |

## Research Outcomes

This collaborative project with Schneider Electric demonstrated significant improvements in AI model development:

- Reduced model training time by up to 45%
- Improved prediction accuracy by approximately 18%
- Decreased computational resource requirements by 30%
- Enhanced model adaptability to changing data distributions
- Simplified deployment process for industrial applications

The developed methodology shows promising applications in various domains including energy management, predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, and industrial process optimization.
