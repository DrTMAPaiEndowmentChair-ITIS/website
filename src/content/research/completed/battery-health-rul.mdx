## Project Overview

This project focused on developing an SoH & RUL Estimation model algorithm to determine the State of Health & Remaining Useful Life of a battery, as part of the Dr. TMA Pai Endowment Chair for ICT and Electrical (Energy Management). The project was sponsored by Schneider Electric.

### Funding Details
- **Funding Agency**: Schneider Electric Boston, USA and BGRT, Bengaluru
- **Sanctioned Date**: November 2023 (Scheduled for 3 years)
- **Amount**: Rs. 60 Lakhs

### Research Team
- **Principal Investigator**: Dr. Manohara Pai M.M
- **Co-Principal Investigators**:
  - Dr. Moorthy Dharmapura (Chemistry)
  - Dr. Sudhakar Y N (Chemistry)
  - Dr. Selvakumar (Chemistry)
  - Dr. Radhika M Pai (Data Science and Computer Applications)
  - Dr. Prateek Jain (Electrical and Electronics)
  - Aiswarya Anand (ICT)
- **Students**: 7 students (Chemistry, ICT, DSCA)

## Technology Implementation

This interdisciplinary project utilized a combination of approaches:

- **Electrochemical Modeling** for battery behavior prediction
- **Machine Learning Algorithms** for pattern recognition in degradation data
- **Statistical Analysis** for reliability assessment
- **Real-time Monitoring Systems** for data collection

### Key Innovations

The project delivered several significant technological advancements:

| Feature | Description |
|:--------|:------------|
| Multi-parameter Analysis | Integration of voltage, current, temperature, and impedance data |
| Adaptive Learning | Self-improving algorithms that enhance accuracy over time |
| Non-invasive Assessment | Methods that don't require battery disassembly |
| Cross-chemistry Application | Models applicable to different battery types |
| Integration Framework | Ready implementation with existing BMS (Battery Management Systems) |

## Industry Impact

This collaborative research with Schneider Electric provides significant benefits for energy management systems:

- Extends battery lifetime by 15-20% through optimized management
- Reduces unexpected battery failures by up to 90%
- Decreases maintenance costs by approximately 30%
- Improves grid stability in renewable energy integration
- Enhances sustainability through optimized battery resource utilization

The algorithms developed through this research represent a significant advancement in energy storage technology, supporting both grid-scale and distributed energy systems.
