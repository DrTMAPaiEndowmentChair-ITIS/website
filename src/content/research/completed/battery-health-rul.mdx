## Project Overview

This project focused on developing an SoH & RUL Estimation model algorithm to determine the State of Health & Remaining Useful Life of a battery, as part of the Dr. TMA Pai Endowment Chair for ICT and Electrical (Energy Management). The project was sponsored by Schneider Electric.

### Funding Details
- **Funding Agency**: Schneider Electric Boston, USA and BGRT, Bengaluru
- **Sanctioned Date**: November 2023 (Scheduled for 3 years)
- **Amount**: Rs. 60 Lakhs
