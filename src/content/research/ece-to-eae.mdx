## Project Overview

This project focuses on developing and implementing sustainable energy solutions, including advancements in solar panel efficiency and smart grid management using IoT.

### Core Areas
- Solar Power with next-generation photovoltaic materials
- Wind Energy optimization through advanced turbine design
- Smart Grid Optimization using AI-powered load balancing

## Research Innovations

Our team is working at the cutting edge of renewable energy technology, with several breakthroughs in development:

- **High-Efficiency Solar Panels** achieving 29% conversion rates
- **Modular Wind Turbines** adaptable to various environmental conditions
- **Distributed Energy Storage** systems for grid resilience
