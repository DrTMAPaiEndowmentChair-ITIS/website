## Pushing the frontiers of AI

A workshop on the Applications of bleeding-edge AI in collaboration with industrial projects was successfully organized by the students of MIT, Manipal in association with the School of Computer Engineering at MIT Manipal. The event featured in-depth discussions on emerging opportunities and advanced techniques in AI, with a particular focus on innovative frameworks such as the Model Context Protocol. Participants gained valuable insights into real-world applications and cutting-edge developments shaping the future of artificial intelligence.
